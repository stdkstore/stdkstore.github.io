SUBEXPONENTIAL

A tense, moody narrative-exploration game from STDK Studio.

Recommended by STDK
Contains thematic drug references
Mild violence

Quiet night, until the sirens pierce the darkness. You thought it was over — they found you. You tried to escape, but it was too late. The crash took everything from you. Your life, your family, your future… gone.

But you survived. Somehow, you’re back. Broken, hunted, and starting from nothing. Tonight, the streets are waiting. We need to get back to the top! You understand this!??

SUBEXPONENTIAL is a psychological horror game where the darkest enemy is yourself. Step into a world of tension and consequence as you navigate the precarious life of a fallen dealer, managing resources, making choices, and uncovering a fragmented, immersive story.

Presented in a first-person, pixelized animated style reminiscent of classic PSX-era visuals, the game blends cinematic storytelling with intense, personal exploration. Every decision matters as you balance survival, ambition, and the haunting echoes of your past.

Dark, gripping, and mature in theme, SUBEXPONENTIAL is recommended for 18+ players due to its depictions of drug use, dealing, and violence. For the fullest experience, play in a dimly lit room and immerse yourself in its tense, atmospheric world.
