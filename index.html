SUBEXPONENTIAL

A tense, moody narrative-exploration game from STDK Studio.

Recommended by STDK
Contains thematic drug references
Mild violence

Quiet night, until the sirens pierce the darkness. You thought it was over — they found you...

But you survived. Somehow, you’re back. Broken, hunted, and starting from nothing. Tonight, the streets are waiting. We need to get back to the top! You understand this!??

SUBEXPONENTIAL is a psychological horror game where the darkest enemy is yourself...

Presented in a first-person, pixelized animated style reminiscent of classic PSX-era visuals...

Dark, gripping, and mature in theme, SUBEXPONENTIAL is recommended for 18+ players...
