The Studio
STDK
Solo · Hobby · Godot

STDK is one person — 17, working a mechanical job, making games on the side. School didn't stick. The path toward becoming a "real" developer closed a while ago, and that's just how it is. But the making never really stopped.

Games get built in the gaps — after shifts, on weekends, whenever there's enough energy left. No team, no budget, no plan to go full-time. Just Godot, a folder of ideas, and enough stubbornness to finish something.

"Not everyone who builds things ends up where they thought they were going. Sometimes you just keep building anyway."
